About Me

Disney World has long been a magical place for those who step through the gates. Sadly, people are passing up on the magic for two main reasons: Too expensive and not having children. I'm here to debunk those outrage thought processes, join the masses and fall back in-love with imagination and possibilities!

So who am I? A self-described "Disney Nerd" with aspirations in supporting Millenials in reclaiming life. 32 years young, I have specialized in all forms of budget friendly vacation planning for the past 10 years.
